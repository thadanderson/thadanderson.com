**Answers** is a quintet of improvising composers. Equally at home in amplified and acoustic settings, the band performs spontaneous improvisations, original works, interpretations of graphic scores, virtuosic contemporary classical music, and works for open instrumentation. Answers is on a mission to perform without limitations.

- Thad Anderson, vibra-kit
- Beatriz Ramirez-Belt, oboe
- Chris Belt, guitar
- Brandon Miller, bass
- Caitlin Pequignot, violin
