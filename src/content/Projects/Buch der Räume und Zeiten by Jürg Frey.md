I recorded the percussion 1 part for Jürg Frey’s work _Buch der Räume und Zeiten_. Below is some further details about the project.

The following description was taken from the project website: [http://bdruz.irritablehedgehog.com](http://bdruz.irritablehedgehog.com/)

Jürg Frey’s _Buch der Räume und Zeiten_ (Book of Spaces and Times) is written for two players who choose from 23 different parts for a performance. This yields 253 possible combinations for the piece.

In December of 2016, [Andy Lee](http://randrewlee.com/) revisited this score and thought it would be interesting to have each part recorded, making a complete recording of the work a possibility. With a blessing from Jürg, the agreement of David McIntire ([Irritable Hedgehog](http://irritablehedgehog.com/)), and the cooperation of artists from the United States, Chile, England, Switzerland, the Netherlands, and Germany, we began work on the project in March of 2017.
