The Lou Harrison Percussion Instrument Collection is a set of instruments owned or made by [Lou Harrison](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lou_Harrison) during his long career as a composer, musician, and artist. It was donated to and is supervised by [William Winant](https://williamwinant.com/), who was a longtime collaborator of Harrison's towards the later portion of his career. The collection is housed on the campus of [Mills College](https://www.mills.edu/) in Oakland, CA.

On multiple occasions, I have had the opportunity to visit the collection and Willie at Mills. The following represents a catalogue of the instruments that I created during these visits. Please reach out to [myself](https://thadanderson.com/contact/) or [Willie](steakatto@hotmail.com) should you have any questions.
