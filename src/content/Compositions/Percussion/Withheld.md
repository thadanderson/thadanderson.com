**Withheld** (2011), a piece from my series called Lines, is a percussion quartet for tuned metals. This particular recording was made with tuned pipes, but it could also be performed with any assortment of tuned metals (crotales, glock, vibraphone, chimes, celeste, bell plates, almglocken, gongs, etc...). It was composed with the pipe pitches used for David Lang’s second movement of _The So-Called Laws of Nature_ in mind. The Lines pieces are based on a series of rhythmic "duration lines," which are used to create both structure and polyrhythmic interest.
