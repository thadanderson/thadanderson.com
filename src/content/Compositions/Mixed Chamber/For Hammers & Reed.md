**For Hammers & Reed** (2013), a piece from my series called _Lines_, is a duet for piano and alto saxophone. It was composed for the Heisler/Yeh Duo and was premiered on November 24, 2013 at the Timucua Arts Foundation "White House" in Orlando, FL.

[Purchase](https://thadanderson.bandcamp.com/merch/for-hammers-reed-2013) the score and parts to perform For Hammers & Reed.
