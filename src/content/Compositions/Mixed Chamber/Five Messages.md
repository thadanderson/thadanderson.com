**Five Messages** (2013), a piece from my series called _Lines_, is a quartet for two pianists and two percussionists. It was premiered by Luke Gullickson, Franklin Gross, Owen Weaver, and myself in March of 2013 at a Collide Contemporary Music Series event at the University of Central Florida. It was later revised to simplify the percussion set-up for a series of performances given by Ensemble P4 in 2015 and later recorded by that ensemble.
