M.O.D.E., standing for musical optical dome experience is a live immersive musical experience, performed at the Museum of Arts and Sciences to launch the Macon Film Festival on August 16th, 2018. The experience included 360 immersive visuals by [Diana Reichenbach](https://dianareichenbach.com), which I accompanied with a live musical performance. The full performance involved four short films, two of which I had composed the music for, [Sonolumin](https://thadanderson.com/Compositions/Multi-Media/Sonolumin) and [0.1.](https://thadanderson.com/Compositions/Multi-Media/0.1.). I added live percussion and electronics along with the fixed media component. A full performance from my perspective can be viewed below:
