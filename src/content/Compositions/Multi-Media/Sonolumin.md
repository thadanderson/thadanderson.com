**Sonolumin** (2018) is an original soundtrack created for an animated 360 fulldome film by [Diana Reichenbach](http://www.dianareichenbach.com/).

The premiere was given at event called [M.O.D.E.](http://www.dianareichenbach.com/params/group/105512/), which stands for _musical optical dome experience._ This was a live immersive musical experience, performed at the Museum of Arts and Sciences to launch the Macon Film Festival on August 16th, 2018.

> "The experience included 360 immersive visuals by Diana Reichenbach, accompanied by a live musical performance by composer and percussionist Thad Anderson. The project was made with support from the Knight Foundation and the Museum of Arts and Sciences in Macon, GA."

This project was awarded "Best Sound Experience" at IMMERSPHERE fulldome festival in Brazil in December 2021.
