## PEREGRINATION: An intimate journey through visual and sonic landscapes

**Peregrination** / ˌpɛr ɪ grəˈneɪ ʃən /  
noun  
1. to travel from one place to another; journey.  
2. to voyage through or over; traverse.

**Thad Anderson,** _composer & performer_  
**Chris Brannan,** _film editing and visual effects_  
**Diana Reichenbach,** _film editing and visual effects_  
**Benjamin McGonnel,** _aerial cinematography_**  
David Randag,** _aerial cinematography_

PEREGRINATION is a multimedia work combining stunning landscape cinematography and live video processing along with composed electronic audio and live improvised percussion elements. Intended to take the viewer on a journey from the comfort of their home, this project is a collaboration designed for presentation using modern video conferencing platforms (Made for Pandemic™). It was presented eight times over four nights from November 30 through December 3, 2020.

Each premiere event had a limited digital audience gathering to allow for intimate conversation and viewing experience. The artists provided background information on the collaborative process, production details, and their vision for the project. After each presentation of the work, there was a Q&A period and a general discussion between artists and audience.
