Percussionist, composer, & arts leader, **Thad Anderson** is an Associate Professor of Music and Coordinator of the Percussion Studies program at the [University of Central Florida](https://www.ucf.edu). He serves as President of the [Percussive Arts Society](https://www.pas.org) and is a faculty member at the [Boston University Tanglewood Institute](https://www.bu.edu/cfa/tanglewood/).
