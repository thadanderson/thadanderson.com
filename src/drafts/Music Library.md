**Plus Two** is a music library created by Thad Anderson. The collection contains carefully composed music designed for advertising, podcasting, & film clients. The music below can be licensed as-is, can be customized on request, or new music can be commissioned from scratch to fit your needs.

This is a growing catalog of music that is available for non-exclusive license. If you don’t have the time or the budget for an original composition, this is a great way to quickly find the right music for your project. You can browse by keyword or playlist, download tracks for testing, and even purchase a license right in the browser.
