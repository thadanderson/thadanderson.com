For over ten years now, I have been active as a recording engineer and with post production (editing, mixing, and mastering). I specialize working in concert halls and other venues outside of a normal recording studio environment and use a custom mobile recording workstation that can be used in just about any location. Most of my work is focused on recording percussion instruments
