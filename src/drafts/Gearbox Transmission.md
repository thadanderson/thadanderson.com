Long story short, composer Travis Jeffords wrote a piece for solo marimba, three woodblocks, and a resonant metal for me. The piece is titled _Gearbox Transmission_ and the blocks and metal represent “gears” for the marimba (shifting between various rhythmic values). I recorded the audio in May of 2008 and videographer, Ruwan Perera, put together all of the visuals. Enjoy!
