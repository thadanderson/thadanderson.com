**[Buch der Räume und Zeiten by Jürg Frey](https://thadanderson.com/bdruz)**  
Jürg Frey’s _Buch der Räume und Zeiten_ (Book of Spaces and Times) is written for two players who choose from 23 different parts for a performance. This yields 253 possible combinations for the piece.
